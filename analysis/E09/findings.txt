E09: Contract Ablation Findings
=================================

Key Finding 1: Visibility is the most impactful contract field
  - Removing visibility increases cost by an average 108%
  - arvr_stereo sees a 5.7x cost increase (visibility=EXTERNAL_DRAM forces
    4x communication cost multiplier on the 262144-rate sad_matching edge)
  - ai_llm sees 2.2x cost increase (many inter-kernel edges affected)
  - graph_analytics: no change (contracts already use GLOBAL_MEM)

  When visibility is unknown, the compiler assumes EXTERNAL_DRAM, which
  applies a 4x communication cost multiplier (vs 0.5x for LOCAL_SPM).
  This dramatically inflates cross-core transfer penalties.

Key Finding 2: Permission fields have zero effect
  - no_permissions produces identical costs to full_tdc across all domains
  - This is expected: may_fuse, may_replicate, may_retile are advisory
    hints for the TDGOptimizer (E10), not enforced by BendersDriver
  - The compiler ignores these fields during L1 assignment and L2 mapping

Key Finding 3: Dependency-only sometimes performs better
  - dependency_only averages 2.7% lower cost than full_tdc
  - With fewer constraints, the L1 solver has more freedom in assignment
  - arvr_stereo: dep_only=0.673x vs full_tdc (37% cheaper)
  - The absence of rate/visibility info means the solver uses default
    cost model, which happens to produce better load balance for some
    workloads

Key Finding 4: Domain sensitivity varies widely
  - arvr_stereo: most sensitive to ablation (5.7x range)
  - graph_analytics: least sensitive (1.0x to 1.18x range)
  - High-rate edges (like arvr_stereo's 262144) amplify contract effects
  - Low-rate uniform domains (graph_analytics) are contract-insensitive

Domains most sensitive to visibility removal:
  1. arvr_stereo: 5.692x (has 262144-rate LOCAL_SPM edge)
  2. ai_llm: 2.224x (has multiple large-rate edges with LOCAL_SPM)
  3. dsp_ofdm: 1.385x (moderate rates with double-buffered edges)
  4. robotics_vio: 1.164x (small rates, moderate impact)
  5. zk_stark: 1.015x (small rates, minimal impact)
  6. graph_analytics: 1.000x (already uses GLOBAL_MEM, no change)
